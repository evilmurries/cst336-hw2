Place a bet in the text box, and click the pull button. If you get three matching images then you win! Press the reset key to start over. Cherries multiply your bet by 5, bar by 10, and sevens by 25; however, if you lose then it subtracts your bet from your score. You start out with 1000 so have fun, but if you drop to zero then you lose!
